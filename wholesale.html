WHOLESALE & DISTRIBUTION

Connecting Systems for a Global Distributor

The Challenge

Operating across 12 regional warehouses, this global distributor was crippled by fragmented data. Their order management software didn't communicate directly with their warehouse management systems (WMS). This forced staff to manually re-enter order details, causing a massive backlog in dispatch times and leading to frequent, costly shipping errors for their B2B clients.

The Domain-Plus Solution

We architected a unified, cloud-based ERP ecosystem. We built custom API connectors to instantly bridge their sales portals with their warehouse floors. We then introduced automated workflows: the moment a client places an order, the system instantly locates the nearest warehouse with the required stock and automatically generates the picking tickets and shipping labels.

The Impact

The elimination of manual data entry transformed their logistics pipeline. They drastically reduced their fulfillment cycle, allowing them to offer next-day shipping guarantees to their premium clients, which secured several massive new enterprise contracts.
